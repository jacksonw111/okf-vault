> 💡DESIGN[.]md Best Practices
> 
> DESIGN[.]md is quickly becoming one of the most useful files for AI-assisted UI design because it help AI understand your design system well enough to create UI that actually feels like your product.
> 
> A good DESIGN[.]md usually includes your design tokens, brand style, color rules, typography, spacing, and component guidance.
> 
> 3 best practices for creating a strong DESIGN.md:
> 
> 1⃣ Treat tokens as decisions: A token is not just a variable. It defines the role something plays in the interface.
> 2⃣Add reasoning. Don’t just say what a style is. Explain why it exists, when to use it, and when not to use it. This helps AI make better design decisions.
> 3⃣Build components on top of tokens. Components should reference your tokens and design rules.
>
